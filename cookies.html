What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work properly, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

Cookies we use

We may use strictly necessary cookies (required for the site to function), preference cookies (e.g. language or theme), and analytics cookies (to improve our site). We will only use non-essential cookies with your consent where required by law.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect how the website works.
